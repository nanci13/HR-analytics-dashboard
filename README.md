## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze employee data and provide actionable insights into workforce performance and attrition. It helps HR professionals identify trends, monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), and make informed decisions using data visualization.

---

## 🚀 Features

- Employee Count Overview
- Attrition Analysis
- Attrition Rate Tracking
- Average Age of Employees
- Average Salary Analysis
- Average Years at Company
- Attrition by Gender
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Education
- Attrition by Salary Slab
- Attrition by Job Role
- Attrition by Years at Company
- Interactive Filters and Slicers
- Dynamic Charts and KPIs

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

The dashboard provides valuable insights including:

- Overall workforce distribution
- Employee attrition trends
- Departments and job roles with high attrition
- Salary-wise employee turnover
- Education level impact on attrition
- Age group analysis
- Experience and tenure analysis
- Gender-based workforce distribution
